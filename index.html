BIGGEST NAMES IN ANIME

MONKEY D. LUFFY

The adventurous captain of the Straw Hat Pirates from the anime "One Piece," Luffy is known for his dream of becoming the Pirate King. With his strong will and Gum-Gum powers, he has formed a diverse crew and faced numerous challenges on the Grand Line.

NARUTO UZUMAKI

The main character of "Naruto," Naruto is a young ninja with dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. He is known for his determination, resilience, and the ability to harness the power of the Nine-Tails fox spirit.

EREN YEAGER

The protagonist of "Attack on Titan," Eren Yeager is known for his fierce desire to eradicate the Titans and protect humanity. His journey is marked by intense struggles and revelations about his own abilities.

NEZUKO KAMADO

From the anime "Demon Slayer," Nezuko is a demon who retains her human emotions and fights alongside her brother Tanjiro. She is known for her unique abilities and fierce protective instincts.
